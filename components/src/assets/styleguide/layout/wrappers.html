Before you use <ins-header> and <ins-sidebar>, it is important to know how structure things with these wrappers.

Insites Admin <ins-admin>

Main wrapper that holds the whole page body together.

Insites Content <ins-content>

Page content wrapper that holds the content part a page.

Insites Renderer <ins-renderer>

Renders the "iFrame" content of a page. Also automatically manages page's heading & breadcrumbs (up to 1 level deep).
